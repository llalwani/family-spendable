Codename: Shared Bucket

Thank you for participating in testing my app. Please be as thorough as you can when filling out your bucket list, since I need to see how similar or varied people's lists are!

When adding a goal, you will choose between 3 types of goals: Activity, Accomplishment, and Traveling. Activities are things you do only one time, whereas Accomplishments are something you work multiple times for.
For example, "Start a novel" is an activity whereas "Finish a novel" is an Accomplishment.
"Go Snowboarding" is an activity and "Snowboard down a double-black diamond trail" is an Accomplishment!
